About me

I have always felt the need to build things, so naturally, I gravitated towards a degree in Civil Engineering and a career in construction. I felt I enjoyed my job most when I was encountering new problems and learning new techniques or technologies.

This led me to teach myself to code in my spare time and found that I not only had an affinity for it, but I actually found it deeply enjoyable. I decided to take things further by applying for a course at General Assembly and I could not be happier with this decision.

I hope working as a developer will allow me to continue building (apps and websites, rather than buildings!), whilst working with like minded people in an exciting industry. More importantly, I want to learn and be challenged every day.
